What We Do

At Matt's Pharmacy and Stores, we make everyday healthcare simple, accessible, and reliable. Our team is dedicated to providing high-quality medications, trusted health advice, and a warm, supportive experience for every customer who walks through our doors or shops online.

Prescription Services

Fast and accurate dispensing, medication reviews, and guidance to help you use your prescriptions safely and effectively.

Over-the-Counter Medicines

A wide range of products for common health needs, from pain relief and allergies to digestive care and wellness supplements.

Health Consultations

Professional support for managing conditions, choosing the right products, and understanding your treatment options.

Diagnostic & Wellness Services

Blood pressure checks, blood sugar testing, and other quick in-pharmacy screenings to help you stay on top of your health.

Immunizations

Routine vaccinations and preventive care offered by trained professionals in a safe and comfortable environment.

Personal Care & Lifestyle Products

Quality skincare, baby care, hygiene items, vitamins, and daily essentials chosen to support your well-being.
